👨‍🎓 About Me

Hello, I’m Yizhe Yang, an undergraduate at Lanzhou University majoring in Physics with a minor in Management. My research primarily focuses on the development of applied large language models, particularly at the intersection of AI, geography, and atmospheric science, with an emphasis on building AI-driven solutions to current challenges in these fields. I am also involved in related areas of large language model research, including cross-modal alignment, safety alignment, and context engineering.

✨ Research Interests

  • Foundation Models: Large Language Models, Multimodal Models, AI Agents
  • Alignment & Safety: LLM Safety and Alignment, Cross-modal Alignment
  • Applied LLM Systems: Applied LLM Development, Context Engineering
  • AI for Geoscience: Geography- and Atmosphere-oriented LLM Applications
