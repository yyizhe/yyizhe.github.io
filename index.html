About Me

Hello, I’m Yizhe Yang, an undergraduate at Lanzhou University majoring in Physics with a minor in Management. My research primarily focuses on the development of applied large language models, particularly at the intersection of AI, geography, and atmospheric science, with an emphasis on building AI-driven solutions to current challenges in these fields. I am also involved in related areas of large language model research, including cross-modal alignment, safety alignment, and context engineering. I am actively seeking for Phd position and a Research internship in 2027. I am happy to engage in discussions regarding potential opportunities!

Research Interests

  • Applied LLM Development, especially at the intersection of geography and atmospheric science
  • LLM Safety and Alignment
  • Context Engineering for LLMs
  • Cross-modal Alignment
  • AI Agents
  • Multimodal Models
